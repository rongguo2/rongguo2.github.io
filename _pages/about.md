I am a research scientist working on the development of advanced MRI technologies. My research focuses on magnetic resonance spectroscopic imaging, ultrahigh-field MRI, and multi-modal imaging of the brain. The goal is to support earlier diagnosis, more precise characterization of brain disorders, and improved monitoring of therapeutic interventions, by combining innovations in acquisition, reconstruction, computational modeling, and machine learning.
